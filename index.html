Frequently asked questions

How does the AI generate quizzes?

Our AI analyzes the video's transcript and uses natural language processing to identify key concepts, facts, and relationships in the content. It then formulates relevant questions and distills the information into flashcards and summaries.

What happens if a video doesn't have a transcript?

If a video doesn't have captions available, our system won't be able to process it. We'll show you an error message in that case. Most educational and professionally produced videos have transcripts available.

Can I edit the generated content?

Yes! After generation, you can edit any of the questions, answers, or summary content before exporting it to your preferred format.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.
